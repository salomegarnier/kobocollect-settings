KoboCollect Project Settings Generator

Answer a few questions about your data collection workflow. This tool will generate recommended KoboCollect settings for project administrators, including what to enable and where to enable it.

How to use this tool

Step 1 Answer the questions about your project and field workflow.
Step 2 Review the recommended KoboCollect settings.
Step 3 Apply the settings on each data collection device, or configure one device and share the setup by QR code.
